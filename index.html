Interests

I got bit by the cycling bug while at university. Running was taking a toll on my knees and my brother had a mountain bike, so I got one too. I joined the cycling team and promptly fell in love with the sport and all the wonderful folks that it has introduced me to along the way. Since then, I’ve ridden all sorts of different mountain bikes, road bikes, touring bikes, city bikes, cross bikes, fat bikes, gravel bikes, even e-bikes. That is to say, I will rarely say no to a bike ride of any flavor, but dirt is my favorite flavor. Give me a big ole rowdy mountain bike and some rugged trails or a rigid bike with some bags for camping gear and a map, and I’ll be content to ride until the cows come home (or until I’m at home amongst the cows for the night).

Creative solutions to challenging problems seem to flow more freely when I take a break to get outside. I enjoy traveling by any means, especially on foot or via bicycle, which allow a pace suitable for soaking in the scenery, seeing the wildlife, and experiencing the true culture of a place.
